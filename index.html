The Convergence Unravels

For centuries, the Material and Aetheric dimensions existed in delicate balance. Now the boundaries fray.

At Millbrook Crossing, where the Convergence runs deepest, creatures from beyond the veil walk the earth. Forests whisper with voices of the dead. Crystals grow from bare stone, humming with trapped memories. The swamps glow with toxic Aether-light.

An ancient intelligence — the Oblivion Lord — seeks to collapse both worlds into one, remaking reality in its image. The scholars call this the Coming Oblivion.

You arrive at Millbrook Crossing as a stranger. But the Convergence resonates with your presence in ways it does with no other.

The fate of two worlds rests on what you do next.
